A Natural Order

by Yeqian Li

Do people living in the city miss the clear sky when they face the cement forest every day? In rapidly developing cities, nature seems to be inaccessible. In this project, I selected 6 elders from six different places around the world as the main character, who had a lot of experiences. But as they age, they choose to return to nature and live together with rivers, seas, jungles, and flowers. They are the people forgotten by fast-paced life, and they are the people who bravely seek and return to their true selves. Through this project, I want to focus on nature as the theme, with the courage to explore as the main theme. I made it a pop-up book through painting and collages. I have drawn the life of each character who is far from mainstream society and seeks their ideals in nature.
